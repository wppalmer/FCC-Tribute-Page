Timeline of Steve's Career

  • 1976 - Basic design of the Apple I computer is completed with Steve Wozniak
  • 1977 - Apple II computer is introduced at the West Coast Computer Faire
  • 1980 - Has a net worth over $250 million, and became one of the youngest on the Forbes list of the nation's richest
  • 1984 - Introduced the original Macintosh
  • 1986 - Took the computer graphics division of Lucasfilm and made it into Pixar
  • 1987 - Left Apple and found NeXT Inc.
  • 1995 - Pixar releases first feature film Toy Story
  • 1997 - Apple buys NeXT Inc. for more than $425 million, and Jobs returns to become CEO
  • 1998 - Introduction of the first iMac
  • 2001 - The first iPod is released
  • 2007 - The first iPhone is released
  • 2010 - The first iPad is release
  • 2011 - Resignation as Apple's CEO months before passing away
